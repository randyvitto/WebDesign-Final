New Order - Total LP

Originally released in June 2011 on CD, TOTAL from Joy Division to New Order is now available on vinyl for the very first time. Featuring single versions of tracks from both Joy Division and New Order mastered for vinyl by Frank Arkwright from his 2011 remasters. The iconic album cover was created by Howard Wakefield with art direction from Peter Saville.

New Order - Power Corruption and Lies

New Order quashed any theories about the 'tough second album' with Power, Corruption and Lies hitting the top5 in the UK charts. Featuring one of the band's best loved hits, 'Age of Consent', the album is ranked in Rolling Stone's list of the 100 greatest albums of the 1980s

New Order - Movement LP

The debut album from New Order, originally produced by Martin Hannett, who had previously collaborated with Joy Division, features eight original tracks all written after the sudden death of Joy Division's Ian Curtis, including 'Dreams Never End' and 'Truth' which were both penned very shortly in a writing session

New Order - Republic LP

Released 4 years after Technique, and their first since the demise of Factory Records, Republic was the groups second consecutive album to reach the no. 1 spot on the UK albums chart, as well as being nominated for the coveted Mercury Music Prize in 1993. It was originally produced by Martin Hannett
